CSV Format

Required columns:

Column Name Description Example
invoice_number Invoice number (must exist in system) GIT/B/001
installment_no Sequential installment number (1, 2, 3...) 1
due_date Payment due date (YYYY-MM-DD format) 2025-01-15
amount_due Amount due for this installment 933.00
Important Notes:
  • Each invoice can have different numbers of installments
  • Each installment can have different amounts
  • The sum of all installments for an invoice MUST equal the invoice total amount
  • Installment numbers must be sequential starting from 1 for each invoice
  • If an invoice already has installments, they will be replaced
  • If receipts already exist for an invoice, they will be automatically mapped to installments
  • Invoice status will be automatically updated based on receipt payments
